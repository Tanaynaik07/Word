WordTide — Free Daily Word Puzzle Game

WordTide is a free online word puzzle game you can play every day in your browser — no download, no account required. Choose from six themed categories: Nature, Ocean, Food, Space, Animals, and Travel. Guess the hidden word using letter clues, just like classic word games — but with a scoring system that rewards skill, speed, and early correct guesses.

How to play WordTide

Pick a topic, then type a full word as your guess. Green tiles show letters in the correct position. Yellow tiles reveal letters that are in the word but in the wrong spot. Grey tiles mean the letter isn't in the word. You have five minutes and a limited number of attempts — the shorter the word, the fewer guesses you get.

Word of the Day

Every day, WordTide features a special Word of the Day — an ocean-themed mystery word chosen fresh each morning. Win the daily challenge to extend your streak. Miss a day and your streak resets. Sign in with Google to save your streak and score to the global leaderboard.

Scoring and star ranks

WordTide uses a skill-based scoring system. Green letters in your first guess earn +10 points each. Points per green letter decrease with each subsequent guess, down to +2 by guess five. Winning the game earns a completion bonus — up to 1000 bonus points for solving in one guess. A speed bonus adds extra points for time remaining on the clock. Earn ⭐⭐⭐ for a legendary performance, ⭐⭐ for a solid win, or ⭐ for scraping through.

WordTide vs Wordle

While inspired by word-guessing games like Wordle, WordTide goes further: six topic categories instead of one, a skill-based scoring system, a timer, a streak leaderboard, ambient ocean audio, and a daily challenge that refreshes every 24 hours. If you love daily word games and want more variety and competition, WordTide is for you.
